WHY DO I LOVE 6LACK AS AN ARTIST?

6LACK to me is one of the few artist we have in the industry that is very true to himself and it shows through his music. I fell in love with his unique sound and I've been hooked ever since. His play on words are like no other. i close my eyes and his unique sound instantly sootthes me.
